# Lumen Labs — Remote Work Policy (v3, January 2026)

## Eligibility and principles

Lumen Labs is a remote-first company. Every employee may work from any
location in a timezone within UTC-2 to UTC+3. Employees relocating outside
this range must get written approval from their manager and People Ops at
least 30 days in advance.

## Working hours

There are no fixed working hours, but everyone is expected to be reachable
during core hours: 10:00 to 16:00 CET, Monday to Thursday. Friday afternoons
are meeting-free company-wide.

## Time off

Full-time employees receive 25 days of paid vacation per year, plus public
holidays of their country of residence. Unused vacation days do not roll
over past March 31 of the following year. Sick days are unlimited and do not
require a medical certificate for absences shorter than 3 consecutive days.

## Equipment and stipends

Each new employee receives a one-time equipment budget of 1,500 euros for a
laptop, monitor and peripherals, refreshed every 3 years. A monthly remote
stipend of 60 euros covers internet and electricity. Co-working spaces are
reimbursed up to 250 euros per month with a receipt.

## Security requirements

Company data must only be accessed from devices enrolled in our MDM. Public
Wi-Fi may only be used through the company VPN. Two-factor authentication is
mandatory on all internal tools. Lost or stolen devices must be reported to
security@lumenlabs.example within 24 hours.

## Travel and team gatherings

The whole company meets twice a year at an offsite; attendance is expected.
Teams additionally have a quarterly gathering budget of 400 euros per person
per quarter, which cannot be converted to cash.
